City of the Day

Kabul

Kabul is Afghanistan's capital and a crossroads of cultures, with bazaars, traditional crafts and mountain panoramas. Notable sites include the old city markets and nearby historical sites.

Kandahar

Kandahar has a long trading history and cultural significance in the south. The region is known for distinctive local textiles, bread types and communal gatherings.

Herat

Herat features Persian-influenced architecture, especially tilework on mosques and madrassas. It has been a cultural center for centuries.

Mazar-e-Sharif

Mazar-e-Sharif hosts festive spring celebrations like Nowruz and is known for its colorful shrines and markets.

Jalalabad

Jalalabad sits on important trade routes and is surrounded by fruit-producing valleys; local cuisine and hospitality are central to its culture.

Bamyan

Bamyan is famous for its dramatic valley and archaeological niches. The landscape has inspired local crafts and storytelling traditions for generations.
